DEC 13TH

The Opening of Toastmasters at Sweetwaters

By Sweetwaters Coffee & Tea

DESCRIPTION

This club is open and everyone is welcome! Sweetwaters staff and community members are creating a new Toastmasters club @ Sweetwaters. We will have some experienced Toastmasters members on hand too, to show the basic format of a Toastmaster's meeting. Attendees will have the chance to give input into the new club and create a great experience and build a new club culture!

Come a little early and pick-up a beverage or snack, if you would like from Sweetwaters. Have fun making new friendships with encouraging and supportive people! You can be part of the founders of the club!

There is success at Toastmasters! People try new things and everyone wins. It is an encouraging atmosphere of supportive people. When you join Toastmasters - you receive a Mentor and you may want to Mentor other people too. There is free street parking at the meters after 6 pm, and parking in the residential areas by First & Second street. If you are a Toastmaster and would like a meeting role, please feel free to contact me (Isabella), if you would just like to know more about this awesome event, or Toastmasters come enjoy and have fun!

We look forward to seeing you at this exciting event! Everyone is welcome!
